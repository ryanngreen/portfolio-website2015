Warmth: Knitting

This is a side project that I worked on. Knitting is a hobby of mine, but it's always frustrating to have to search across so many sites to find different information. I couldn't help but think how easy it would be if all the beginner information I needed was on one site.

Warmth Pages

The project started with the idea of knitting and what this site/brand could be called. I settled on the logo fairly quickly from there, using the most basic knitting stitch for the logo mark and a calm, relaxed color palette.

I decided to make a homepage have multiple calls-to-action so that the visitor would be more likely to stay on the site and click through to an interior page. The subpages include many sources for information that a beginner knitter might be looking for like different end results they can make while practicing, tutorials on basic stitches and materials, as well as benefits to knitting for people who may be on the fence.

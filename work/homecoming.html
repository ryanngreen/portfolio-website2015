Homecoming Site

Previously, Southeastern University created a new sub-site each year that was specific to that year's Homecoming theme. This meant each year they were repeating the same process and essentially creating a time drain. Instead, we decided to create a site that would remain consistent every year, providing a recognizable look, with exchangable pieces for the theme.

Visit Site

Homecoming Homepage

Services Web Design, Logo Design

The most important information is housed on the main page. It serves as the hub for any information someone might be looking for about Southeastern's Homecoming week and activities. As this is a site that will be live long before Homecoming week, a countdown was extremely important, as well as information about when Homecoming week is, what the theme is, and who is sponsoring it. The semester football schedule can also be found, as well as a photo stream from the previous Homecoming.

The other major page included in the new Homecoming site is the events page. Each day is separated in tabs and updated for the current day of Homecoming week. In addition to being organized by day, each day's activities are easy to look over with a header for times and a description of events.
